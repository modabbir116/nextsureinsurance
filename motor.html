What is Motor Insurance?

Motor Insurance (also known as Auto or Car Insurance) protects you against financial losses due to accidents, theft, fire, or natural disasters. As per the Motor Vehicles Act of Bangladesh, it’s mandatory for every vehicle to have at least a Third-Party Liability policy. NextSure offers comprehensive protection — for you, your passengers, and your vehicle.
